Acclaimed for becoming a recent winner of the Irvine Youth Music Festival, Amati Strings performs publicly at a variety of venues for the greater good of establishing a musical presence within the community. Amati Strings, a determined group of young, gifted musicians, was founded in 2014, following a recital performance at the Vanguard University during the 11th Annual Solo Violin and Viola Recital. Since then, Amati Strings has shared their music with the elderly at a variety of senior centers monthly, including the Atria Senior Living Center and Silverado Tustin Memory Care Community. The attendees to these performances became inspired by the different classical and modern genres of music, as could be seen by a senior standing up out of her wheelchair to sing and clap along to “It Had to Be You.” Amati Strings also made an appearance at the Irvine Civic Center to perform for Mayor Steven Choi and the rest of the City Council Members during the Irvine City Council Meeting.
